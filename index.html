About

Scaling Today is not a demand, but an exploration of the state of research and development that is currently being iterated on so that the Ethereum blockchain can achieve mass adoption. Far from being a definitive source, the goal of this project is to highlight best practices to aid developer onboarding, specifically as they relate to scaling through Casper, Sharding, Plasma and State Channels.

Scaling Today is a free and open source community project made possible by the May 2018 Ethereum Foundation Grants program.
